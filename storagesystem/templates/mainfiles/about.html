Welcome to storage facility

Here we help students to store there luggage with highend security enclosed containers.We offer both private container units and shared container units.For the private units,you have complete control over the lock on your storage unit. while for the shared units, the keys are kept at the facility but you can access them at any day of choice as long as it is within the normal working hours. Access to the compound is only possible within stated working hours and only by registered and identified people associated with your unit. The sign in procedure & access to storage units is supervised by the facility security personnel and recorded on the CCTV system.
